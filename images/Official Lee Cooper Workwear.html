Our Proud History

Lee Cooper, the original British jeans company, has now been trading for over 100 years and is a classic. A clothing and retail company that maintains in its rightful high profile position at the very forefront of leading brands. When the dynasty began in the early 1990's, its roots were firmly planted in denim work wear. We now present to you our modern take on the brand history: a collection of work wear clothing and safety footwear. Whilst maintaining the high quality image and heritage the brand has held for over 100 years, our safety work wear products are not only modern and trendy, but are all compliant to EN standards and safety measures, to ensure you are kept looking stylish AND safe on the job.
